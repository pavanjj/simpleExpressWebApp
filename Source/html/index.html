About this page

This is a website that is in initial stages of being built. This is meant to solve a problem I faced a repeatedly in Bangalore - problem of searching for good rented houses. I looked online for a portal that could give me good reviews on a place I was trying to rent - and guess what ! There was none. It would be so good if a credible source could tell us about the actual living conditions we might experience once we move in!- like owner-friendliness, peacefulness, neighbours etc. When we look for reviews for even a small commodity we buy online, then why not for a house which, if we move into will becomes our home for foreseeable future
